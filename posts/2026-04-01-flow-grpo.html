应用案例：Flow-GRPO Use Case: Flow-GRPO

2026年4月1日 April 1, 2026 · 阅读约 5 分钟 5 min read

在 Flow-GRPO 论文中，采样想加入随机性，就必须得将 reverse 过程改造成 SDE。在原文中：

$ \mathbf{f}_{SDE} = \mathbf{v} + \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2} \nabla \log p_t(\mathbf{x}) $

非常简单可以看出来这就是上面我们将 ODE 转 SDE 的简化形式，其中 $\mathbf{G}(\mathbf{x}, t) = \delta_t$。

以这样的视角来看，很快就理解了。

In the Flow-GRPO paper, if we want to introduce randomness into the sampling process, we must transform the reverse process into an SDE. In the original text:

$ \mathbf{f}_{SDE} = \mathbf{v} + \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2} \nabla \log p_t(\mathbf{x}) $

It is very easy to see that this is simply the simplified form of converting ODE to SDE as mentioned above, where $\mathbf{G}(\mathbf{x}, t) = \delta_t$.

From this perspective, it becomes very quick to understand.
